Account and Data Deletion Request

How to request deletion of your Lauburu Grappling Map account and the data associated with it.

How to request deletion

Email support@lauburugrapplingmap.com with the subject line:

Delete my Lauburu Grappling Map account

Email a deletion request

What to include in your request

  • The email address associated with your Lauburu Grappling Map account.
  • (Optional) Whether you want only certain data deleted, or the entire account.

What gets deleted

On a verified deletion request, we delete or anonymise the data associated with your account, including:

  • Account profile and authentication record.
  • Health metrics imported or synced through the app (Apple Health, Health Connect, WHOOP, Polar, CSV imports, manual entries).
  • Training session history, AI Coach context, feedback submissions, and preferences tied to your account.

What may be retained

We may retain limited data when retention is required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or payment-record reasons. Any retained data is kept only as long as required and is not used for any other purpose.

How long it takes

We respond to deletion requests within 30 days. Most requests complete within a few business days.
